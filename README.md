# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

An end-to-end HR Analytics project built in Power BI, focusing on hiring performance, recruitment channels, workforce distribution, and cost analysis.  
Designed to help HR teams and business leaders make data-driven decisions with clear, interactive visuals.

---

## 👨‍💼 Designed by **Advait Muglikar | Business & Data Analyst**

---

## 📌 Project Purpose

Human Resource teams deal with large datasets—hiring costs, recruitment channels, joining time, relocation expenses, job roles, and demographics.  
This project transforms raw HR data into a set of **insightful, interactive dashboards**, enabling fast and informed decision-making.

The dashboard answers important HR questions such as:

- Which regions are hiring the most employees?
- How much are we spending on hiring and relocation?
- Which recruitment channels deliver the best results?
- What are the monthly and yearly hiring trends?
- How is the workforce distributed by age and job role?
- Are hiring costs increasing over time?

---

## 🧰 Tech Stack

- **Power BI Desktop** – Data modelling & dashboard creation  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation & shaping  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Measures for KPIs, ratios & calculations  
- **Data Modelling** – Relationship building between employee & store datasets  
- **File Formats** – `.pbix` (Power BI file), `.csv` & `.json` (data sources), `.png` (screenshots)

---

## 📂 Data Used

The project uses HR hiring and employee data, including:

- Hiring costs (monthly & yearly)
- Recruitment modes (referral, social networks, search engine, magazines)
- Regions & states (East, West, Central, South)
- Job roles (Finance, HR, Manager, Sales, Supply Chain)
- Employee demographics (Age, Age Category, Location)
- Relocation cost, CTC, joining period & interview data

Data files included in `/Data` folder:

- `Employee Data.csv`  
- `Employee.json`  
- `Hiring Data before2023.csv`  
- `Hiring Data after2023.csv`  
- Store-level datasets for all 4 regions

## ⭐ Key Features / Business Impact

### 📌 **1. Executive Summary (High-Level HR Overview)**
- Total Hires  
- Total Hiring Cost  
- Total Relocation Cost  
- Cost per Hire  
- Days to Join  
- Workforce breakdown by **Age Group** & **Job Role**  
- Hiring cost by Region & Job Title  

🧠 **Business Value:** Quick leadership-level insights for HR heads & directors.

---

### 📈 **2. Hiring Trend Analysis**
- Monthly hiring cost trends  
- Year-over-year hiring/outreach comparisons  
- Hiring cost seasonality  
- Trend lines for quick interpretation  

🧠 **Business Value:** Helps identify peak hiring periods & budget forecasting.

---

### 🎯 **3. Recruitment Channel Analysis**
- Cost by recruitment channel  
- Channel effectiveness (hires vs cost)  
- Channel market share  
- Combination charts (line + bar) for deeper insights  

🧠 **Business Value:** HR can shift budgets toward high-performing hiring channels.

---

### 💰 **4. Relocation & CTC Analysis**
- CTC comparison across regions  
- Relocation cost trends  
- Combined relocation vs hiring cost over years  
- Bubble charts for region-level cost mapping  

🧠 **Business Value:** Helps optimize relocation policies & compensation strategy.
